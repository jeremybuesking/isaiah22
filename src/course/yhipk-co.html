Your Heavenly Identity: Priest & King

Dr. Theresa Phillips

My Courses Your Heavenly ID: Course Outline
Session 1

A Royal Priesthood

Every believer has a right to access this part of Christianity. But not all will embrace it.
Session 2

Understanding the Heavens

If history can teach us anything, it should be this: What works for man does not always work for God. A democracy is not the method of the kingdom of God; He demands to rule by a theocracy.
Session 3

The Palace

When it comes to protocol in the palace, we must first realize that you cannot call yourself to the palace, you must be invited or called to it! Once you are in it, you have entered another world completely apart from the outside.
Session 4

The Priesthood of God

We must remember that royal is an inherited position to fulfill the government of God in the land and embrace His divine will.

Session 5

Consecration

Consecration is the act of oneself or itself also known as self-abasement; set apart for service, willingly changed forever. It is a necessary ingredient in the priesthood.
Session 6

Aaron the Priest

"And no man taketh this honour unto himself, but he that is called of God, as was Aaron" (Hebrews 5:4, KJV).
Session 7

Zadok the Priest

The priesthood is an official part of the house of David, a royal priesthood in action. Zadok and Abiathar acted as high priests on several important occasions.
Session 8

The Order of Melchizedek

"And Melchizedek king of Salem brought forth bread and wine: and he was the priest of the most high God" (Genesis 14:18, KJV).
Session 9

The Transfer of Power

From Aaron, to Levi, to Melchizedek, to Jesus, the transfer of power for the priesthood has been transferred to us through a royal bloodline. ... But does a royal bloodline count today?
Session 10

The Demands of a King

Yes, every King will have his demands! He demands honor, respect, submission and glory. He demands praise and worship. He demands victory in His realm!
Session 11

A Matter of Honor

The power of giving and receiving honor is very difficult for many in the American church. Coming from a heritage of true rebellion toward tyranny, we see the American church suffer from a lack of showing honor.

Session 12

Living in the Monarchy

To live in the heavenly monarchy, we need to learn to express and posture ourselves for the coming Christ and the King of glory.
