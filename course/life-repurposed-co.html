Life Repurposed

Dr. Thom Gardner

My Courses Life Repurposed: Course Outline
Session 1

Who do you think you are?

Many people spend much time looking their past and miss the present with God. Our lives can resemble the old school game of Pick Up Stix with a brightly colored chaos behind us. Lesson One deals with the realities of how we see ourselves.
Session 2

What is in your hand?

Our human instinct is to select only the positive accomplishments our lives have produced. We seldom openly value the challenges of our own personal times in the wilderness.
Session 3

Throw down your sticks

Lesson Three will demonstrate the process of God’s redemption of what we may not always see as valuable when they are presented to God. How do my own flops and failures become powerful in God?
Session 4

Destiny from the Dumpster

This lesson will include stories for life and also the Scriptures of God’s redemptive process. They will include, Moses, David, Ruth, Us…
Session 5

Swallowing up the Power of the Devil

There are inevitable voices who are willing to echo our past to us. The devils says, “Show me something.” This lesson will give us the process of putting our wilderness in the hands of a compassionate Father.
Session 6

Leading with Compassion

Who do we trust? This lesson will show us how redeemed sticks can make effective leaders through turning sticks into the staff of God.
Session 7

Overcoming Impossible Odds

All of us face seemingly impassable and impossible obstacles along the way of our journey. With the enemy behind us and the Red Sea in front of us we learn to “stand and see” God’s power revealed through our repurposed past.
Session 8

Living Water from the Rock: The Master Stick

In this lesson we will look at spiritual warfare and growth in our growth in God. We will battle the Amalekites and bring water out of the Rock with the Master Stick: the cross of Christ.
